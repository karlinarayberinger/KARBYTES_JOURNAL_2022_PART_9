KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_SUFFERING

It is approximately 2:30PM and I am back in my latest favorite camping spot near Lake Chabot. I only got about two hours of sleep last night because I was busy working on my projects and because I had to walk away from the 580 freeway in Dublin because the auditory harassment combined with the freeway noise was getting unbearable to me. After I woke up and charged my electronics at the “power tower”, I rode the BART train back to Castro Valley and walked up Redwood Road while talking to myself incessantly because my head was (and still is) filled with other people’s voices talking over my own internal monologue such that I feel smothered, raped, and tortured to a severe extent (while doing my best to not look like I am freaking out in public). I have decided that the police are severely abusive to me and are my biggest enemies (even though they have seemingly allowed me to get away with petty theft and other petty crimes). I think the police have been impersonating my ex boyfriend in order to terrorize me and to prevent me from enjoying myself as much as I otherwise would. I blame the police for why I do not have a job. I think the police bombard my brain with electromagnetic signals which prevent me from being able to process my thoughts and experiences such that I am sleep deprived, unable to retain information as effectively as I otherwise would, unable to think in a way which would allow me to actually calm down and feel hopeful about the future, and be in such a bad mood that it is seemingly physically impossible for me to hold down a job (because the police force me to be too mentally overloaded with harassing content to handle even the most basic jobs). I conclude that I am homeless because the police bullied me out of house and home and that I am deprived of money because the police intercept incoming donations and sabotage my efforts to get and to keep a job. It seems that the police want to keep me in a hostage situation in which I am psychologically and physically tortured in plain sight and made to look crazy to the general public.

I am saying this so that other people know that I am not getting treated well by the police. I am not getting any special favoritism by the police. In fact, in many ways, I am being treated far worse than the average civilian is (by cops and by civilians). People seem to think that I deserve to be punished at least as badly as I have been (and I do not quite understand why so many people seem to condone that I be forced to suffer against my will so intensively). I see civilians playing cop and following me out to where I camp to bully me and to deprive me of quietude and privacy. That is the main reason why I have settled for being permanently misanthropic, friendless, and willing to engage in criminal activity.

I recently saw a video clip on Instagram in which Bernie Sanders talks about how the world is currently in emergency mode due to climate change and environmental collapse. I have felt that sense of urgency and emergency mode for years. I wish I could get the general public to care more about the environment, but the vast majority of people seem smugly indifferent. I could go on and on to cite facts about how bad for the environment the beef industry and the fossil industry are, but people seem quick to drown me out with ad homimen insults and non sequiturs. People seemingly would rather focus on superficial subjects and sensationalist scandals than talk about what I think is humanity’s biggest priority: switching to ecologically sustainable infrastructure as quickly as possible.

As I write this, I am being harassed by that female voice which hiss whispers, whimpers, and coerces my attention to focus on banal and wasteful subject matter. After I publish this note, I intend to take at least 20 minutes to sit in “silence” and let that cacophony of mind rapists litter my mind with their trash and see if any of my real ego is allowed to rise up and contribute original thought.

I expect to die before I am 70 years old (most likely by suicide). My faith in humanity doing what I think is ethical is very low. I do not think I am physically allowed to be happy nor healthy. All I have to seriously look forward to and be happy about is getting my primary website (Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com) as up to date as possible and engaging in hobbies which pertain to the subject matter in that website. In other words, I am my only friend (and I am not looking for additional friends). I am my own spiritual guru (yet I sometimes seek advice from other spiritual gurus and public intellectuals). I feel coerced into becoming increasingly secretive about what I do with my life because I seem to get punished when I disclose “too much” information about my activities, plans, feelings, and ideas. As much as I try to get along with other people and to make them like me, I see that the police force me to be in a perpetually bad mood such that I feel that I am forced to be socially distant and outwardly stoic. I am distressed over how my ego seems to be cornered and forced to commit suicide so that a fake persona can take over and hijack my body and pretend to be me. At this time, I feel very under seige and abused. This might be the last time I publish anything this personal on the Internet again.

One last thing: I have not checked my business PayPal account yet to see if anyone donated money to me through the PayPal donation button link on my website. I am tentatively planning on waiting at least one week before I do. After that, I plan on unpinning the social media post which asks people to donate $1 to me to help me test the link to make sure that it works. I am quite pessimistic that I will see any income from this. In the past, no one donated any money when I asked people to on social media. It seems that people are not willing to donate even a single dollar although it is hardly an inconvenience to them. I think people just want to spite me and/or to make me feel punished for failing to conform to how they think I ought to behave. Perhaps I will be pleasantly surprised and see that some people will have donated money to me through my website a week from now. I am already bracing myself for the disappointment I expect when I login to my PayPal business account and see that no one contributed a penny.

(As I attempt to add a new page to both my WordPress sites using various browsers, the button is not working. Now it is, but I fear it will fail on me again. I think the cops are trying to impede my work because they want me to feel stolen from. Maybe I should just pack up my things and go back to Dublin. The voices in Castro Valley are worse than the ones in Dublin. Apparently, I am not allowed to thrive in Castro Valley. I must have offended the people here).
