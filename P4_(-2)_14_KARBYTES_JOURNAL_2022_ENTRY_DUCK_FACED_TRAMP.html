I saw a thumbnail image on one of the recommended articles which showed up when I opened my Firefox web browser (a news aggregator called Mozilla Pocket) and thought that image was a perfect depiction of the kind of caricature which I am most turned off by and who resembles the most annoying voice which invades my mental space. She has a huge, neonatal head, an apathetic expression, a childlike body, and a snooty attitude. Her behavior reminds me of what a capybara is: neither affectionate nor playful nor intelligent but just good at hanging out with other animals as a lethargic blob with a bitchy expression. When she talks, she hardly projects her voice nor enunciates her words. She sort of mumbles while sighing in a “been here, done that” kind of irritability. (She is the Castro Valley voiceover I hate most. I prefer the shrill wailing Dublin voiceover to that one). That caricature talks in a soft, uninspired, and self righteous way with a smug and passive tone and appearance. She seems to dampen my fun and to talk in a way which sounds very passive aggressive yet “chill”. She suggests that I am too “high strung” when I just seem to be more mentally active, passionate, and concerned about the environment and about existential philosophy than she is. She seems content to just wait for things to happen to her and to hardly do anything adventurous. She reminds me of what I am really starting to detest in black and brown people who seem to be taking over my life and acting like little chauvinist brats who insinuate that I am a Nazi, white supremacist, and immature child just for having nerdy interests and for preferring to spend my time alone in the wilderness rather than in urban environments where social studies is the only subject matter allowed. I do not meant to be racist (and I do not think I am genuinely racist nor sexist, but I seem to live in a time and place where “people of color” may age act like apathetic bimbos and the guys think they are funny, clever, and cool when they come across to me as lame, idiotic, belligerent, entertaining, unfunny, and attention whorish while “their” women shriek with exaggerated laughter from the sidelines). One need to only look at video clips from World Star Hip Hop Instagram to get a taste of what I am talking about. I feel like these overgrown adolescents are the people I have to pretend to emulate and admire in order to get a job that is not white collar. I have worked with such people and found them to be unfriendly, shallow, and bland. I am mentally preparing to have to kiss their ass (but I will not go overboard to do so). I expect them to not like me and to be rude to me while I just assume a cold, stoic, robotic demeanor and just go through the motions and say the canned phrases I have to in order to earn my keep at Taco Bell or whatnot.
