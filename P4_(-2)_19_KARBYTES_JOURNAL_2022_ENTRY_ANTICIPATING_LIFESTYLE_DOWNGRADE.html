KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_ANTICIPATING_LIFESTYLE_DOWNGRADE

As I was walking back to the bush where I left my water bottles and bags of cereal which I purchased yesterday, the guy on the bike appeared again and said he had something to give me. I stopped and watched him open up his bag which had some grapes and peaches in it. When I said no thanks to the offer he commented that it must be because I am turned off by the fact that he is a Mexican. (Something tells me he knows about what I have been doing for the past few weeks and some of the things I have said out loud while talking to myself (because I like to narrate my own life for fun) and the stuff I wrote on the Internet). I kept walking and he followed me to the bush. He told me he was on parole and currently homeless and that he recently got robbed of his truck by the police because he was a criminal suspect (and that he was a car mechanic and had a personal business fixing cars). He continued to follow me as I made my way to the BART station. He said that I looked mad. Then I fessed up that I was mad and thought he was annoying. I said some mean words and flipped him off (and then he stopped following me). I do feel guilty for being mean, but I also feel guilty for having lived like a homeless person in front of actual homeless people while having the option to go home. I have a mom who has been giving me money every day and I have not been very grateful about it and have had the audacity to criticize her. I do think that people dislike me for valid reasons. I appear to be a very selfish, spoiled, and sheltered person; but I am trying to improve. I feel bad for the guy I flipped off and said mean things to and I do feel sorry for him that he is in the situation he is in, but I really do not want to hang out with him nor take on his problems. Perhaps he works for the police as an undercover cop and was doing his job: getting me to return home so that I am not such a public burden. At this point, I am headed to my dad’s house (which is my current legal address) because that is where my shampoo, hair conditioner, laundry detergent, and extra clothes are located. Perhaps I should just keep camping up at Lake Chabot campgrounds. I seem to have to travel far away from the streets in order to avoid getting harassed. Why not stay home? People get mad when I camp in the backyard. They just want me to stay indoors and to not loiter outside. Why do I not just do what they want? I just want to be alone. That’s why. Other people live in my house and I feel micromanaged by my neighbors like they want me under house arrest. I intend to keep living outdoors in secluded locations even though that gets in the way of me holding down a job because it is the only way I seem to be able to work on my website without feeling like I am being placed under house arrest and forced to play mouse next to my neighbors. People probably do not want me camping outside, not even in my backyard, because they do not want me to be a liability. They seem to think I am going to get beaten up, stolen from, raped, kidnapped, or murdered unless I am inside a building with locked doors. Well, I argue that I am not my neighbors’ problem to worry about. I am a full grown adult and I am making an effort to stay miles away from their houses so that I can live my own life without being accused of cramping their style (and vice versa). They seem to think that I am not doing enough to look after my parents and to make them feel that I am not putting myself in harm’s way. Well, if I did what my neighbors seem to want, I would feel that I am under house arrest for their convenience and not free to talk to myself as loudly as I want to and free to enjoy the solitude I can only get while far enough away from where other people hang out and live. I can tell that society is rigged against me. I have to go against the grain of what is normal to live the kind of life I want. The world feels so militaristic and oppressive. It’s like women are not allowed to live alone nor go places by themselves. It’s like everyone has to be part of a group at all times and, if they are not, there are treated as villains. People ask me whether I am a guy or a girl sometimes and I just tell them I am both (or else I let them decide one way or the other). I feel like a criminal just for wanting to be a loner in public rather than hiding in a house I only leave for a few hours at a time at most. It’s like people have to know where I am at all times or else they freak out. That’s not how my parents are. That’s how I imagine other people are. People seem to want to know where my money comes from and how I am able to afford to live as well as I do. Who paid for me to have this nice backpack and laptop? Who pays for me to have nice food to eat? Who pays for my mobile phone service? If I had no money, eventually my phone plan would run out and I would have to use a Wi-Fi in order to access the Internet. If and when this laptop craps out, I would have to use one of my two spare laptops. Basically, the real question is: when am I going to get a job so that I can replace the stuff which will inevitably need to be replaced sooner or later? I do not know. Right now, I think it is a waste of time to worry about getting a job. I should just hold onto what I have for dear life and avoid being in crowded public places as much as possible. Apparently, I am a bad influence on the children. Apparently, I do not serve other people enough nor take enough interest in their lives. There is no way they would want to hire me until I am not so stressed out and angry looking. I am not sure if I will ever be able to exude a pleasant and gregarious demeanor. That’s the real reason why I am afraid I am not able to get a job. There is stuff I am trying to do which takes a lot of time that I want to get done before I resume job searching. Can people please just let me get my shit done so that I can apply for jobs not having that dark cloud hanging over my head or is that too much to ask for? It is too much to ask for. Otherwise, people would leave me alone. People want me to give up having a website and just focus on getting a blue collar job and not being so stressed out and antisocial. I cannot do that without getting a lobotomy nor do I want to. I will keep fighting to do what I want to do even if other people try to stop me because I think it is the path of least resistance ultimately.

So I decided against moving back to my mom’s side of Castro Valley because it is too close to the street. I decided that I am going to have to spend hours walking in one direction just to get a place to use my laptop where I can feel secluded yet not cut off from Internet. I do not think I will be going back to Dublin any time soon because I am afraid that guy on parole is there and will try to talk to me again. So my world is already shrinking around me. The walls are closing in. I already feel deprived of space to think. My head already feels broken into and invaded by other people who want me to feel more downtrodden and urbanized. I think other people have a fetish for raping a pristine unicorn. People seem to like seeing me feel like a soiled dove; someone who used to be innocent, naive, and living in a childlike bubble enjoying nerdy interests but who is now being deprived of such things because “the world” demands that I be more “worldly” for their sake. That’s the real reason I say I want to commit suicide at times; because I am being deprived of the opportunity to live the kind of nerdy lifestyle which means the world to me (and I can tell almost no one but me cares). I do not want to have to change myself to fit in with other people who are not as enchanted by nature and not as engrossed in the subjects I am interested in. Other people seem to insist that I be prevented from enjoying my hobbies so that I give up and settle for conforming to the same “misery loves company” society as seeminlgy everyone else has settled for being a prisoner of. Compared to me, other people seem money obsessed, jealousy-driven, mean, apathetic to science, and socially conformist. Is it a crime to have no friends nor to want friends? Apparently, yes. Apparently, I do not deserve to be happy unless other people are the gate keepers to my happiness.

It feels like I need to refresh my identity somehow. I cannot easily change my appearance nor erase evidence of my past. In many ways, I am a prisoner of other people’s expectations of me. I can seemingly only go as far as they allow me to. I apparently am forced to conform to what they want and expect. Of course I am in a bad mood until I figure out some way around this. I keep thinking that I am going to have to induce a real ego death in myself so that I can live with more of a genuine and lasting sense of peace. I just do not see how that is possible without renouncing my ambitions and personal standards. I feel that I am being rushed to go as minimalist as possible, to get back to petroleum dependence, to get back to living indoors, and to hardly have interests outside of mere socializing with whoever I interact with on a regular basis. It is really a crime to be an introvert who prefers to be alone rather than with other humans. This is no exaggeration. I expect that I will have to really force a smile in order to get a job and that I will, sooner or later, get a reputation for being in a bad mood and not wanting to talk to other people as much as others think I should. For that reason, I am considering just settling for full time package handling (and driving the petroleum van to and from work because the hours are long and the warehouses are usually far away from BART stations). In other words, I predict that I am going to have to become a sellout sooner or later and pretend that whoever wrote all these “pink bin” journals is getting ready to die soon to make way for someone else to take over and say, “Chillax! There’s plenty of time to fix the environment. Even if there isn’t, no one lives forever, so come on! Get on the money making machine already so that you don’t have to be so deprived of basic necessities and respect from other people. You really cannot enjoy your life until you do. You don’t want to miss out on having nice things because you are opposed to fossil fuels dependence.”

If and when I sell out and become another gasoline consumers, I will let you know via this journal website. Until then, I intend to travel gasoline free. (Right now I am at Castro Valley BART station about to make my way up to my dad’s house to take a shower and to wash my clothes).

As I write, annoying disembodied female voices taunt me and laugh sadistically and say raunchy things so that I feel deprived of the right to be as puritanical as I want to be. I am forced to live in a world which is predominantly ghetto, raunchy, and anti-introversion. Of course I feel like the last of my kind. I feel like a wounded animal who is not allowed to heal and who has to limp through life while being abused without any hope for a better standard of living. It’s like I am not even allowed to have goals. It seems that I have to be poor, depressed, and in pain for the rest of my life. Perhaps it is better if I just accept that this is as good as my life will ever be and not expect it to get any better.

I cannot be as peaceful and happy as I want to be. I cannot have as fun of a life as I want to have. I cannot be as productive as I want to be. I cannot have the kinds of friends I would prefer to have. I seem forced to settle for being micromanaged by catty, shallow, mean, anti-introversion bimbos. It will be a miracle if I am able to get my website as up to date as I want it to be. Hell, even getting one more page of that site up to date is a miracle (and if I do get even one more page of Karlina Object dot WordPress dot com up to date, I will have to suffer a lot to do so because other people insist on making me feel punished for doing anything I care about which is counter to their agenda).
