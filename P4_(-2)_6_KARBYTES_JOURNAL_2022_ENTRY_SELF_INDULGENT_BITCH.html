KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_SELF_INDULGENT_BITCH

While charging my electronics at “the power tower” in Dublin/Pleasanon, I stared at the gray concrete ceiling while feeling the cool summer breeze grace my sun burnt skin and listening to the constant rush of nearby freeway traffic. It’s a pleasantly modern environment with wide open skies and sprawling flat lands which I have come to call my alternative hangout spot to the places I usually go in Castro Valley. I started coming here approximately two years ago after trying to secretively live at California State University East Bay campus in Hayward. Once I discovered that Dublin BART station parking garage and the nearby Persimmons shopping center had free public electrical outlets, I felt that I had come across a gold mine and that I would no longer have to play hide and go seek at CSUEB campus. The nearby Whole Foods Market has very good free public Wi-Fi as well and I get almost all of my electronics shopping done at the nearby Best Buy and Target. Basically, I have come to discover that Dublin/Pleasanton is a very nice place to get almost all of my basic needs met. Hence, I tentatively plan on staying in this region until I get the first eight pages of Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com up to date.

Rather than announce each updated web page on my Twitter and Minds feeds, I decided to just update all eight pages first and then, once all eight pages are in their “final draft” mode, share the updated pages on my social media feeds. I do not want to drive traffic to my primary website until all eight pages are up to date. Then, after that, I can announce each additional page which I add to that website without there being “under construction” pages remaining on the site. I am trying to get to a point where I can sincerely say that I am living as though each day were my last so that, if I am unable to finish any additional pages, my website will not look so incomplete because only complete pages will be displayed on that site.

I am having a bit of a battle with myself as I get the first eight pages up to date. Part of the reason why I am having a hard time is that I feel insecure about how I have to listen to propaganda which law enforcement beams into my skull. Though I find such bombardment increasingly tolerable, I still, at times, feel very worried that I will never be able to get to listen to my own thoughts without such interference “corrupting” my otherwise puritanical thoughts. I am worried that, if I do not hurry up and get my website up to date, I will be forced to take a job before I can and then not have enough free time between work shifts to relax, study, and give my website the painstaking attention to detail it deserves because I will be too brainwashed and drained from my mind-numbing, soul-crushing, socially-conformist job. (Yes, I know that I will probably have to get a job before the end of the year and I sincerely plan on doing so closer to the holidays. I have severe anxiety and a sense of chosen despair due to my belief that not enough is being done to mitigate global climate change and ecological toxicity and destruction. I see that, in order to get and keep any job, I have to be rather apathetic to such concerns. That makes me not want to apply for jobs at all. Feeling like I am relatively alone in my concern for the environment has made me contemplate suicide daily. Of course, I do not want to commit suicide nor harm any person. Also, I know that from experience that it is most pragmatic to pretend that I am always okay because showing the signs of mental illness is a barrier to employment and preventing other people from imposing on my personal choices under the justification that I am deemed too mentally ill to make decisions on my own behalf).

I see that, just being an intellectually curious, thoughtful, and ambitious person is a setup to be ridiculed, ostracized, and bullied into submission by social conformity mongers. Hence, I am practicing not talking to myself out loud and not sharing social media posts which I think make me look mentally unstable to other people. I understand that I have to give up my youthful ideals of always speaking my mind and not filtering what I have to say such that other people to not find what I have to say controversial or upsetting in any way. In the past, I used to pride myself on not tailoring my personal expression to make myself more socially acceptable to the masses, but recently, I have started to accept that it is in my best interest to just keep most of my thoughts as private as possible and to avoid drawing attention to myself as much as possible. In fact, I pride myself on being more stoic, self reliant, and solitary than the average human adult. While other people cry and whine about how underappreciated, anxious about the future, and disadvantaged they feel, I pride myself on being a lot less needy, whiny, and manipulated by culture into adopting a “basic bitch” or “macho gangsta” persona (which I see many boys and girls my age exhibiting on social media and “in real life”). I am trying to minimize sharing how disgusted and disturbed I am by how robotic, unoriginal, and trite many people my age seem to be brainwashed into acting. That’s a huge reason why I plan on being mostly sycophantic at whatever my next jobs are; being careful to remain relatively impersonal, bland, and tight lipped so that I do not give people the wrong idea. I am not going to work to find people to have sex with nor to hang out with outside of work. I am just trying to spend as little time as possible worrying about money so that I can spend as much time as possible studying physics, computer science, and philosophy in my spare time and working on my website (which I intend to showcase my passions and expertise in such topics).

Of course I have lost sleep over the prospect that nothing I do will be sufficient to prevent the human species from going extinct due to environmental neglect (especially as a result of continued mass reliance on fossil fuels and the cattle industry). I have settled for assuming that I will likely get cancer or some other fatal ailment as a result of being exposed to environmental toxicity and the stress of living in a barbaric society. I imagine that I am going to become terminally ill or else severely crippled before I reach 50 years of age. If I can help it and my future seems to bleak for me to justify continuing to survive for, I plan on committing suicide by jumping off a very tall cliff. My fear is that I will be incarcerated or otherwise physically prevented from being able to commit suicide in my preferred way. Hence, I feel that it is very worth my time to establish some kind of Buddhist-inspired meditation/spirituality practice (especially after I complete the first 8 pages of my “personal bible” of a website (i.e. Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com) so that I can maximize how much I enjoy my life no matter what the circumstance).

Of course I have lost sleep over the prospect of my website getting tampered with or censored off the Internet. My personal philosophy is supposed to help me cope with impermanence, mortality, and a sense that I might not profit from my investments. Perhaps I will make several M-disc backups of my website and keep those optical discs in a metal safe in my bedroom for my peace of mind. (The data which is inscribed onto an M-disc is supposed to last for up to 1,000 years).

Well, thank you for kindly listening to what I have to say on the matter of my life’s work. I know this all may seem very selfish, self indulgent, and not worthy of other people’s investment. I do not mean to be negative nor to make anyone feel bad. I am just not very good at making people feel good. As a philosopher who actually enjoys thinking about depressing, morbid, and very serious and heavy subjects, I do not think I appeal to the masses because the content I generate is not designed to make people feel good. It is designed to satisfy my soul’s deepest cravings for a sense of closure about what I think would otherwise be dismissed as unfinished business, incommunicable concepts, or else issues to cover up with unsatisfying and anthropocentric platitudes which discourage skepticism and politically incorrect emotions. I am not content to glibly parrot others that not all aspects of reality can be known nor explained in clear, concise English. As pretentious, grandiose, and even megalomaniacal this sounds, I endeavor to come up with my own grand theories of everything and to ensconce my best formulated theories in my personal website. Thank you for allowing this journal entry to take residence in your attention span. Time to go!
