To Whom It May Concern:

My name is Karlina Ray Beringer and the pronoun I identify with is “it” (which means I identify as neither a man nor a woman and that I intend to become a cyborg which lives for 700+ years).

While walking down Santa Maria in Castro Valley today and passing the village shopping center, I thought I overheard some little boys on bikes saying that I am too shallow to get a job. Rather than argue against what they said, I will simply pretend that I agree with them because it seems that people would prefer to cherry pick evidence to support the idea of me which most serves their ego rather than what is true. I could suggest many reasons to counter the accusation that I am too shallow to get a job, but I think it is futile to argue with people who stubbornly cling to their cozy narratives of reality and end up wasting my time and energy as a result.

As shallow and random as this may sound, I have heard many people over the years suggest that I am implicitly required to pack on visibly more subcutaneous fat than what is currently on my body. Otherwise, my lean body suggests to other people that I am too attached to appearing physically fit to be a good employee, girlfriend, babysitter, and mother. That layer of fat would make me look more cuddly, effeminate, erotic to heterosexual men and fat fetishists in general, and advertise that I am “low maintenance” and do not mind being in “energy conservation mode” by not having to exercise much nor consume much food (and that I can handle being in chronic starvation mode which signifies that I am “appropriately destitute”, deprived of free time, deprived of nice places to workout, and forced to alternate between starving and binging due to the fact that, as a poor person, I am not able to get in more than one or two meals per day).

I want to inform the public that I am aware that what I said above could be entirely made up and fed to me as propaganda by people who want me to have a good enough motivational story to tell myself so that I am not such a miserable, lazy burden to put up with. I will just tell the public that I pretend that most other people want me to be more sedentary, endomorphic, fat-ridden, and matronly so that they do not feel envious of my sleek and agile physique in a world which makes it hard for people to eat a plant based diet, burn approximately as many calories as one consumes, and make physical and mental fitness as high of a priority as I make it. (I was also told earlier today that many people disapprove of me being a student when I do not have to be (and that being a student is a criminally self indulgent thing for me to do at this stage in my life)).

I like to pretend that people are as fucked up as I describe because it motivates me to not be as fucked up as I pretend they are. I am a very egotistical, vain, and self-centered person who wants to maximize its self esteem even at the cost of fitting into society, having friends, and making money. For someone like me, it feels like a waste of time to hang out with people and to mimic their lifestyles. I would prefer to make personal development the center of my life and make doing health related and creativity related personal experiments take up most of my free time (and the reason why I would bother getting a job).

I understand that I am not doing enough to conform to the tradition of passing the torch onto the generation after me as soon as possible by having kids as soon as possible. Rather than rush to make motherhood my highest endeavor in life, I selfishly refuse to have children and refuse to get into a committed relationship so that I can focus on investing in my own development instead of in nurturing the next generation of humans to attain a higher level of personal development than I ever would given my sacrificial role.

Even though I am not supposed to worry that my children or their children (or their grandchildren) might not survive ecological collapse on Planet Earth, I am supposed to hurry up and have as many babies as I can afford so that, while my kids are in my custody, I get to enjoy the perks of being a mother. Once my kids become adults, I am not supposed to be too concerned about what becomes of them.

So, I am just here to compete with the younger generations for space to exist, resources to consume, and positions of privilege to attain (instead of get out of their way and live a much more modest and frugal lifestyle than they would). I do imagine that plenty of people would prefer that I commit suicide than attain success, happiness, and well being during my lifetime because they are jealous of how good I have had it compared to them and because they do not want me to continue having such a good life.

So, until I rush to get a job in customer service or in food service, I am being lazy, stubborn, racist, and selfish. Until I feminize my appearance and downplay my wit, strength, egotism, and intelligence in order to make chauvinist men feel better about themselves, I am being a traitor to my family, friends, and community at large. It’s not their fault that I am taking so long to get a job or to complete tasks on my to-do list. It’s entirely my fault for procrastinating and blaming those people for why I procrastinate. I will just use the insanity clause to excuse myself to continue making up bullshit reasons to not do what other people (or even what I) think I ought to have finished by now. I will just tell them that I am not mentally fit enough to be taken seriously and that I am probably not eligible for most jobs yet.

Thank you for letting me vent about my life to the general public. I know free speech is not something every person is entitled to. If I have it, I am blessed.
