Karlina is not having a good time. Other people seem happy about that fact (and Karlina knows it).

No matter what time of day or night it is and no matter where Karlina seems to go, other humans seem to be preventing Karlina from being able to get away from hearing the sounds other humans make. What distresses Karlina a lot is having other people inject their words into Karlina’s mind such that Karlina’s authentic thoughts are suppressed and Karlina is literally forced to think someone else’s thoughts using their words instead of Karlina’s. As a writer and philosopher, not being allowed to think one’s own thoughts is very disturbing to Karlina and makes Karlina seriously consider committing suicide. Karlina is afraid that Karlina will be incarcerated for months in a mental hospital and forced to take mind suppressing drugs against Karlina’s will if Karlina attempts suicide. Also, Karlina does not want to commit suicide before Karlina’s primary website, Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com, is in its final draft form.

Karlina is afraid that the police and others are deliberately attempting to deprive Karlina of all the things Karlina values such as being able to attain profoundly deep and satisfying states of consciousness through meditation and psychedelic use, the ability to enjoy computer programming and to engage in it almost daily, the ability to earn money without having to forsake Karlina’s ideals, a place where Karlina can go to enjoy privacy and not having to be intruded upon by other people, the ability to maintain a stable routine, the ability to travel outside the Bay Area, the right to recieve donations through Karlina’s websites, the ability to make money online and to use the Internet reliably at home, the right to study physics and mathematics without being bombarded with distracting and discouraging noises, the right to resume taking testosterone injections to masculinize Karlina’s appearance and to improve Karlina’s sense of gender identity dysphoria, freedom from being harassed and bullied in public and in private, and the right to be well rested and able to enjoy quality sleep and relaxation activities.

In summary, Karlina concludes that Karlina is not allowed to be happy because the government and civilians do not want Karlina to be happy and that Karlina probably never will be happy and that Karlina’s life is going to become increasingly unsatisfying to Karlina as Karlina ages.

Karlina is fairly certain that humanity will go extinct within the next 300 years due to environmental neglect, false scarcity of resources, and warfare. Karlina also expects to die before Karlina reaches 100 years of age and that, unless Karlina dies by well executed suicide, Karlina will be forced to die a slow and excrutiating death due to some disease such as cancer which is caused by being exposed to sufficiently high amounts of harmful radiation and environmental toxicity which Karlina seems unable to avoid. Karlina thinks that, if Karlina loses a limb of major sense, no one will be willing to help Karlina get prosthetic limbs or to get restorative surgery to replace lost functions because not enough people value Karlina’s autonomy and happiness and too many people take sadistic pleasure in seeing Karlina deprived of autonomy and happiness.

Karlina is afraid that Karlina is being discouraged from setting and achieving goals, from talking out loud to itself (especially due to the fact that the world Karlina lives in is overpopulated and Karlina is being coerced into limiting its mobility and minimizing how much noise it makes so as to not disturb the people who are deemed to matter more than Karlina), from looking good, from feeling good, from having things to look forward to, from having good things to remember, from being able to do sufficient research to be as informed and supported as Karlina prefers to be, from being able to meet and interact with people who would make Karlina feel appreciated and inspired, from having a dog, from having a private residence, from having a career, and from having hobbies.

Karlina believes that Karlina is forced to be chronically angry and extremely anxious. Karlina is afraid that Karlina will spend most of its life suffering and hardly any of its time feeling satisfied. Karlina thinks that Karlina is being forced to look like and feel like a rape victim without being shown compassion. Karlina thinks that people want Karlina to feel underpaid, underappreciated, ignored, misunderstood, vilified, set up to fail, stolen from, tortured, corrupted, and driven to self destruction.

Karlina feels that Karlina has nothing to offer humanity (and Karlina’s desire to serve humanity is waning). Karlina thinks that Karlina is not allowed to promote any agenda other than settling for being an underachiever, hermit, misanthrope, liar, sycophant, slave, abuse victim, scapegoat, and entertainer to the low brow masses.

Karlina’s preferred way to commit suicide is to jump off of Half Dome cliff at Yosemite National Park. Even though Karlina seems to be forbidden and physically prevented from setting and achieving goals, Karlina is trying to become as stoic, detached, rational, and intelligent as possible. Karlina is trying to minimize how many worldly attachments it has so that Karlina has as little to worry about attaining and maintaining as possible.
