KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_LIFE_IS_A_GAME

I have not yet left my dad’s house to go to some camping spot. Also, I seem to have found the cause of me getting booted off the Internet. I was sitting too low on the garage floor such that the washing machine and/or dryer on the other side of the wall seemed to be blocking the Wi-Fi signal. If I stand up and place the laptop on top of the mini fridge in the garage, the signal is much clearer. I can even listen to music videos while I type!

It sure would be convenient if I could just delete all the pages in the “pink bin” which I find cringe worthy and only keep the pages which make me look good, but all of those pages are backed up to Archive dot Org (and probably some government database). I might as well keep them in tact on this website.

Most of the “pink bin” consists of content which I find to be reactionary in response to being harassed by other people, but some may say that it is a big bloated diary showing how mentally ill I am and how lacking in “real world” accomplishments I am.

If I could sum up what the “pink bin” is about in one word, I would say PROCRASTINATION. If I could sum up what the “pink bin” is about in one sentence, I would say, “Karlina is documenting Karlina’s journey to becoming what Karlina wants to be rather than what Karlina feels coerced into being against Karlina’s will (and the many struggles which come with that journey).”

I am disgusted that so much of Karbytes For Life dot WordPress dot Com does not even begin to cover subject matter I find cool, interesting, or worthy of revisiting. It feels like something I did to make myself feel better while feeling that I was playing a game whose rules are rigged against me and designed to make me look bad to the outside world. It looks like I used to have an easier time doing what I want to do before psychotronic harassment took over my life and made me feel like a hostage and scapegoat. I am disgusted at what a VICTIM I sound like throughout most of this website. Maybe I should just delete most of the “pink bin” pages so that, when people look through the actual website table of contents MENU button in the upper right corner (which displays each website page in alphabetical order) and the most recent backups of this website on Archive dot Org, they will only see the pages which I want people to see.

I do not want people to have to spend in inordinate amount of time having to dig through this website to find the stuff I think represents what I want to be remembered for. There is too little “green bin” content at this point and too much “pink bin” content. Also, the server space for this website is almost filled to capacity with “pink bin” content. Unless I delete most of that stuff, the “pink bin” content will take up significantly more space on this website than will the “green bin” content.

But I spent a lot of time amassing this “pink bin” content in the hopes that, once I thoroughly address some issue, I will not have to do it ever again. The “pink bin” exists to show that I went through plenty of hazing rituals and personal inconvenience in my life pertaining to sexism, racism, classism, and logistical and physiological barriers to attaining my goals. I am hoping that, after this note, I will not have more than ten pages left to contribute to the “pink bin”. I am hoping that I am close to being able to move on fully into the “green bin” and never have to add stuff to the “pink bin” ever again.

Should I delete anything I have already published on this website? I might. I might even delete this note. Maybe the best thing I can do with my time tonight is delete lots of stuff from this website. I just need a place to do it where I do not have to waste so much of my mobile data and where I will not feel overwhelmed by the psychotronic harassment.

That high pitched female voice keeps telling me something like, “All you care about is yourself, Karlina. No one likes you. All you do is dew code. Excuse whore. All you do is play with your boyfriend. You’re only allowed to pee and poop. At least I don’t have to talk about my ex. Give me something new to talk about.”

I logged into my PayPal business account and saw that no one has apparently donated anything to me even though I asked on three social media accounts. Oh wait, never mind. A friend I know in person donated a dollar. Awesome! That seriously made my night. (I will just delete those e-begging social media posts right now then because all I needed was to confirm that the donation link was functional).

Rather than delete anything I have already published on this website, I think it would be best to keep it. If the server space for this domain name runs out of space for “green bin” content, then I will just make a third WordPress account and make that third website link to this website and use that third website as the KARBYTES link on the Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com website.
